Baroque Music Style

  • Baroque music had a lot of style in them and was unpredictable at times
  • Baroque music was very embellished/ornamented (grace notes, trills, etc.)
  • Composers had bad working conditions and were treated as servants
  • Harpsichord was the primary keyboard instrument in this era
  • Composers used polyphonic texture, which referred to many voices in a piece
  • Music mirrored the art and architecture of the time
