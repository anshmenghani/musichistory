Pre-Baroque Music Style

Medieval Time Period (0-1400):

  • Music notation slowly evolved and became refined
  • Numes were used instead of modern-day musical notation
  • The musical staff and clefs were developed

    • Renaissance Time Period (1400-1600):

  • Music began to move away from strictly religious music
  • Music became to be used as art and entertainment
  • Text painting began to be used. It was a style of music where the music reflected the tone/mood and the phrases of the lyrics to the accompanied song
