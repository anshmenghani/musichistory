Musical Manifesto!

Ansh Menghani

Music is rich in history, and since the dawn of life, sounds have been ever-present in this world. This music history study website will help you learn about the ways humans have manipulated sounds to make music from the years 0-present. It features an interactive popup study tool (like flashcards), a (goofy) music history lesson video, and a 50-question quiz to test your knowledge.
