Contemporary Music Style

  • Many composers experiment with new musical ideas
  • John Cage invented the prepared piano, a piano whose sound could be modified by changing the material used to create the sound
  • Atonality; refers to music with no real tonal center
  • Minimalism and minimalistic music appeared in this era
  • Aleatoric music; chance music appeared. This was music where the performer got to decide many aspects of a piece (i.e. imporovisation)
  • Jazz, Pop, Wind Band, and Rock music styles were created and popularized. The invention of the saxophone and euphonium also occurred in this era
