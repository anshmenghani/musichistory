The rise of land based empires occur during this time period. The Mongol (nomadic) empire unties much of Afro-Eurasia (covered around 22% of all land on earth) through Pax Mongolica. Trade develops (SIlk Roads, Indian Ocean Trade Routes, Trans-Saharan Trade Route) and many innovations are shared which leads to population growth and advanced technology.
