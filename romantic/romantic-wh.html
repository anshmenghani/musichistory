  • Romanticism vs. realism
  • Romanticism: attraction to the exotic and unfamiliar, recent advancements (telescope, electric light, battery, etc.). Individualism and feelings (ex. Frankenstein)
  • Realism: painting a portrait (literally and figuratively) of the world around you without romanticizing the tough times (ex. Oliver Twist)
